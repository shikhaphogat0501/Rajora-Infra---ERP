# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Overview

This folder contains the **Business Requirements Document (BRD)** for the **Rajora ERP – Enterprise Residential Construction Management System** project.

The BRD defines the business objectives, project scope, stakeholder expectations, business requirements, and high-level functional expectations identified during the Business Discovery and Requirement Elicitation phases.

It establishes a shared understanding between business stakeholders and the implementation team, ensuring that the proposed ERP solution aligns with the organization's operational goals and business processes.

The BRD serves as the primary business reference for all subsequent project deliverables, including the Functional Requirements Document (FRD), User Stories, Wireframes, Database Design, Testing, and Power BI Dashboards.

# Objectives

The Business Requirements Document aims to:

- Define the business objectives and project scope.
- Capture high-level business requirements from stakeholders.
- Document the business problems addressed by the ERP implementation.
- Establish a baseline for Functional Requirements and Solution Design.
- Align business expectations with the implementation team.
- Support development, testing, User Acceptance Testing (UAT), and deployment activities.
