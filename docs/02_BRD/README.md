# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Overview

This folder contains the **Business Requirements Document (BRD)** for the **Rajora ERP** implementation project.

The BRD captures the business needs, objectives, scope, and high-level requirements identified during the Business Discovery phase. It acts as the primary reference for solution design and ensures that all stakeholders have a common understanding of the expected business outcomes.

This document serves as the foundation for subsequent project deliverables, including the Functional Requirements Document (FRD), User Stories, Process Models, Database Design, Test Cases, and Power BI dashboards.

## Objectives

The Business Requirements Document aims to:

* Define business objectives and project scope.
* Capture high-level business requirements from stakeholders.
* Document current business challenges and expected outcomes.
* Provide a baseline for functional and technical design.
* Ensure alignment between business stakeholders and the implementation team.
