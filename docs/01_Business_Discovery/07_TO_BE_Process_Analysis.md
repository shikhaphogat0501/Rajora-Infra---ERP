1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to define the future ("TO-BE") business processes for Rajora Infra Homes after implementation of the Rajora ERP system.

The document describes how business operations will be transformed through process standardization, workflow automation, centralized data management, and real-time reporting.

This serves as the primary reference for preparing the Business Requirements Document (BRD), Functional Requirements Document (FRD), User Stories, Wireframes, and Technical Design.

2. Future Business Vision

Rajora Infra Homes aims to implement an integrated ERP platform that connects all business departments into a single centralized system.

The future state will provide:

Centralized business database
Automated workflows
Digital approvals
Real-time dashboards
Mobile-enabled field operations
Standardized business processes
Role-based system access
Automated notifications and alerts
Improved customer experience
Faster management decision-making
3. Proposed ERP Solution

The ERP solution will integrate the following business functions:

13. Success Metrics

The ERP implementation will be considered successful if it achieves the following:

90% reduction in duplicate data entries.
70% reduction in manual reporting effort.
50% reduction in purchase approval turnaround time.
100% digital labour attendance capture.
Real-time visibility of construction progress.
Automated MIS generation for all departments.
Improved customer follow-up compliance.
Enhanced management decision-making through dashboards.

The TO-BE analysis defines the target operating model for Rajora Infra Homes following the implementation of the Rajora ERP system. This document provides the future-state vision that will guide the preparation of detailed business requirements, functional specifications, solution design, and implementation planning.
