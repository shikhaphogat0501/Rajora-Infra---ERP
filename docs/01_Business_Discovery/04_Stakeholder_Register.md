1. Purpose

The purpose of this Stakeholder Register is to identify all individuals, departments, and external parties involved in the Rajora ERP implementation project. It documents each stakeholder's responsibilities, level of influence, communication requirements, and expected involvement throughout the project lifecycle.

This register serves as a reference for stakeholder engagement, communication planning, requirement validation, and project governance.

8. Stakeholder Risk Assessment
Risk	Impact	Mitigation Strategy
Delayed requirement approvals	High	Schedule recurring review meetings and obtain early sign-off
Resistance to ERP adoption	High	Conduct training sessions and change management workshops
Limited stakeholder availability	Medium	Plan meetings in advance and share agendas beforehand
Conflicting departmental priorities	Medium	Escalate unresolved issues to the Project Sponsor
Incomplete requirements	High	Validate requirements through workshops and formal reviews
9. Conclusion

The Stakeholder Register provides a structured view of all individuals and groups involved in the Rajora ERP implementation project. It defines stakeholder roles, responsibilities, communication requirements, influence levels, and engagement strategies to ensure effective collaboration throughout the project lifecycle.

This document will be updated as new stakeholders are identified or as project responsibilities change.
