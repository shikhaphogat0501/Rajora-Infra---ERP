1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to record the discussions, decisions, observations, and action items identified during the Business Discovery and Requirement Elicitation phase of the Rajora ERP implementation project.
These meeting minutes provide a traceable record of stakeholder inputs and serve as a reference for preparing the Business Requirements Document (BRD).

Meeting Type	Project Kick-off
Participants

Agenda
•	Understand current business operations. 
•	Discuss project objectives. 
•	Identify existing challenges. 
•	Define ERP vision. 
•	Identify key stakeholders. 
Discussion Summary
•	Business operations are currently managed using multiple Excel files. 
•	Reports are prepared manually by different departments. 
•	Customer information is duplicated across several files. 
•	Management lacks real-time visibility into construction progress. 
•	Purchase approvals are handled manually. 
•	Labour attendance is recorded in physical registers. 
•	Inventory updates are delayed. 
•	Executive dashboards are not available.

•	Digitize manual business processes. 
•	Replace Excel-based reporting. 
•	Standardize departmental workflows.
