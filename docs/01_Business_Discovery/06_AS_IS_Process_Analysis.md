1. Purpose

The purpose of this document is to analyze the existing ("AS-IS") business processes followed by Rajora Infra Homes before ERP implementation.

The objective is to understand current operations, identify inefficiencies, document manual activities, and establish a baseline for designing future business processes.

This document provides the foundation for the TO-BE Process Analysis and Business Requirements Document (BRD).

2. Current Business Environment

Rajora Infra Homes currently manages its residential construction projects using a combination of Microsoft Excel, paper registers, email communication, WhatsApp messages, and manual approvals.

Business data is maintained independently by each department, resulting in fragmented information and inconsistent reporting.

The company lacks an integrated system for monitoring customer bookings, construction progress, labour deployment, procurement, inventory, and financial transactions.

16. Summary

The AS-IS analysis confirms that Rajora Infra Homes currently operates through disconnected Excel spreadsheets, paper-based records, and manual workflows. While these processes support day-to-day operations, they create duplicate data, reporting delays, approval bottlenecks, and limited visibility across departments.

The analysis demonstrates the need for an integrated ERP solution that centralizes business data, automates workflows, standardizes reporting, and enables real-time decision-making. The findings documented in this report establish the baseline against which the future TO-BE processes and ERP solution will be designed and evaluated.
