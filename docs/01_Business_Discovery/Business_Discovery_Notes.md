Company Overview
About Rajora Infra Homes
Rajora Infra Homes is a regional real estate development company engaged in planning, constructing, and selling residential housing projects. The organization focuses on delivering quality homes while managing all aspects of construction, customer relationships, procurement, labour management, and financial operations.
The company is currently developing Rajora Estate, a gated residential township consisting of 125 duplex homes.
The company manages every stage of the customer lifecycle—from lead generation and booking to construction, financing, and final possession.

 Current Challenges
The existing manual system presents several operational challenges.
Data Challenges
•	Duplicate customer records 
•	Duplicate vendor entries 
•	Multiple Excel versions 
•	Inconsistent naming conventions 
•	Missing customer information 
•	Manual data entry errors 
 
Reporting Challenges
•	Reports prepared manually 
•	Delayed MIS generation 
•	Time-consuming data consolidation 
•	No automated dashboards 
•	Limited historical analysis 
 
Operational Challenges
•	No centralized database 
•	Labour attendance tracked manually 
•	Inventory updates not in real time 
•	Manual purchase approvals 
•	Delayed communication between departments 
•	No automated notifications 
 
Management Challenges
•	Lack of real-time project visibility 
•	Difficulty tracking construction progress 
•	Limited visibility into customer collections 
•	No centralized KPI monitoring 
•	Slow decision-making 
 
ERP Objectives
The primary objective of Rajora ERP is to digitize and integrate all business operations into a centralized enterprise application.
Business Objectives
•	Eliminate manual Excel-based processes. 
•	Create a single source of truth for operational data. 
•	Improve reporting accuracy and consistency. 
•	Enable real-time visibility across departments. 
•	Reduce manual effort through automation. 
•	Improve communication between teams. 
•	Support faster and more informed decision-making. 
•	Standardize business processes across departments. 
•	Improve customer experience through better tracking and transparency.

Conclusion
The Business Discovery phase has identified that Rajora Infra Homes currently relies on disconnected Excel sheets and manual processes to manage core business functions. These practices create inefficiencies, duplicate data, delayed reporting, and limited operational visibility.
The proposed Rajora ERP system aims to integrate all major business functions—including Sales, Customer Management, Construction, Labour, Inventory, Procurement, Finance, and Reporting—into a centralized platform.
