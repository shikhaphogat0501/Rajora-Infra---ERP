# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Business Glossary is to establish a standardized vocabulary for everyone involved in the Rajora ERP implementation project.

Using common terminology helps improve communication, reduce misunderstandings, and maintain consistency across business requirements, functional documents, testing activities, training materials, and project discussions.

---

# 2. Business Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Lead | A prospective customer interested in purchasing a property. |
| Enquiry | Initial communication received from a potential customer. |
| Prospect | A lead that meets the company's qualification criteria. |
| Opportunity | A qualified sales prospect with a high likelihood of purchase. |
| Follow-up | Scheduled communication with a prospective customer. |
| Customer | An individual or organization purchasing a property. |
| Customer Master | Central repository containing customer information. |
| Booking | Reservation of a residential unit by a customer. |
| Booking Amount | Initial payment made to reserve a property. |
| Allotment | Official assignment of a property unit to the customer. |
| Cancellation | Withdrawal of a confirmed booking. |
| Agreement | Legal document defining terms between buyer and builder. |
| Possession | Formal handover of the completed property to the customer. |
| Unit | Individual residential property available for sale. |
| Project | Residential development undertaken by Rajora Infra Homes. |

---

# 3. Construction Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| BOQ (Bill of Quantities) | Document listing materials, quantities, and estimated costs for construction. |
| Milestone | Significant stage of project completion. |
| Foundation | Base structural component of a building. |
| Plinth | Portion of the building between ground level and floor level. |
| Superstructure | Portion of the building above the plinth level. |
| Work Order | Formal authorization to perform construction work. |
| Site Engineer | Engineer responsible for supervising daily site activities. |
| Site Supervisor | Individual responsible for coordinating labour activities. |
| Quality Inspection | Verification that completed work meets required quality standards. |
| Snag List | List of defects identified before property handover. |
| Completion Certificate | Official confirmation that construction has been completed. |
| Occupancy Certificate | Government approval permitting occupancy of the building. |
| RERA | Real Estate Regulatory Authority governing real estate projects in India. |
| Architect | Professional responsible for building design and planning. |
| Contractor | External party responsible for executing construction work. |

---

# 4. Procurement & Inventory Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Purchase Request (PR) | Internal request to procure goods or services. |
| Purchase Order (PO) | Official document authorizing procurement from a vendor. |
| Vendor | Supplier providing materials or services. |
| Vendor Master | Central repository containing vendor information. |
| Quotation | Price proposal submitted by a vendor. |
| Goods Receipt Note (GRN) | Document confirming receipt of purchased materials. |
| Inventory | Materials available in stock. |
| Stock | Quantity of materials available for use. |
| Stock Transfer | Movement of inventory between storage locations. |
| Material Issue | Distribution of materials from inventory for project use. |
| Material Receipt | Recording of received inventory into stock. |
| Reorder Level | Minimum stock quantity that triggers replenishment. |
| Warehouse | Storage location for construction materials. |
| Inventory Adjustment | Correction of stock records after verification. |
| Stock Audit | Verification of physical inventory against recorded stock. |

# 5. Finance & Accounting Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Invoice | Document requesting payment for goods or services. |
| Receipt | Confirmation of payment received. |
| Installment | Scheduled payment made by the customer. |
| Outstanding Balance | Amount remaining to be paid. |
| GST | Goods and Services Tax applicable in India. |
| Ledger | Record of financial transactions. |
| Accounts Receivable | Amount due from customers. |
| Accounts Payable | Amount payable to vendors. |
| Cash Flow | Movement of cash into and out of the organization. |
| Budget | Planned financial allocation for a project. |
| Cost Center | Department or project against which costs are recorded. |
| Expense | Cost incurred during business operations. |
| Refund | Return of money to the customer. |
| Financial Reconciliation | Matching financial records for accuracy. |
| Bank Disbursement | Release of loan amount by a financial institution. |

# 6. ERP & Business Analysis Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| ERP | Enterprise Resource Planning system integrating business processes. |
| CRM | Customer Relationship Management module. |
| Dashboard | Visual summary of important business information and KPIs. |
| KPI | Key Performance Indicator used to measure business performance. |
| Workflow | Sequence of business activities performed to complete a process. |
| Business Process | Series of related activities that achieve a business objective. |
| Business Requirement | High-level business need identified by stakeholders. |
| Functional Requirement | Description of system functionality required to meet business needs. |
| Non-Functional Requirement | Requirement defining system performance, security, or usability. |
| Stakeholder | Individual or group involved in or affected by the project. |
| Requirement Elicitation | Process of gathering and documenting stakeholder requirements. |
| BRD | Business Requirements Document. |
| FRD | Functional Requirements Document. |
| User Story | Requirement describing functionality from a user's perspective. |
| Acceptance Criteria | Conditions that must be satisfied before a requirement is accepted. |
| Requirement Traceability Matrix (RTM) | Document linking requirements to design, development, and testing. |
| Change Request | Formal request to modify an approved requirement. |
| Gap Analysis | Comparison between the current and desired business state. |
| AS-IS Process | Existing business process before ERP implementation. |
| TO-BE Process | Proposed business process after ERP implementation. |
